Take a Closer Look

I am a translator and interpreter, mostly Japanese to English and English to German. I primarily translate commercial and promotional websites, advertisements and articles. I also have a fundamental knowledge of Japan, the Japanese culture and society, as well as Japanese video games and games overall. I love character development in writing and also like to translated dialogue-centric pieces. Currently, I am looking to branch out into translations of video games and mangas.

I also have experience in creating web content and graphical design, e.g. writing articles, creating landing pages and web design, photography, creating unique business cards, posters as well as vehicle graphics. Check out some of my work below.

I have a fundamental knowledge of asia and especially Japan having lived and worked for more than 14 years in Japan. I know the Japanese and the European working culture by heart and got what it takes to lead and manage translation and design projects from start to end.

Having lived in ten prefectures plus throughout Japan, I also have a fundamental knowledge of the Japanese geography. I know my way around by any means of transportation, from ferries to planes, I am an experienced hiker and have fundamental knowledge of the Japanese mountains as well as hiking trails.

If you are looking for a translator, interpreter, a tour guide who is able to prepare an unique and unforgettable travel experience, or a designer with special knowledge in Asia/Japan, don't look any further and contact me.
