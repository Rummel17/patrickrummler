About Me

"I am translator, interpreter as well as a writer. I also love to do designs. See samples of my projects below."

Take a Closer Look

I am a translator and interpreter, mostly Japanese to German and English to German. I primarily translate commercial and promotional websites, advertisements and articles. I love character development in writing and also like to translate dialogue-centric pieces. Since I love Japanese video games and also like to read Manga, I am looking to branch out into those areas.

I also have experience in creating web content and graphical design, e.g. writing articles, creating landing pages (e.g. this one), web design, photography, creating unique business cards, posters as well as vehicle graphics. Check out some of my work below.

Having lived and worked for more than 14 years in Japan in 10 + prefectures, I have a fundamental knowledge of the Japanese culture overall and especially the Japanese business culture, as well as the Japanese society. I love working in international exchange e.g. doing presentations and lectures on topics such as differences between Japan and other countries, latest developments in e.g. Germany or Europe, alternative energy, school systems in Europe and many more. During my time in Japan, I have done more than 100 + presentations in Japanese and have planned and organized cultural exchange events, such as visits to Japan e.g. of exchange students, organized homestays etc.

If you are looking for a translator, writer, an expert in international exchange, or a designer with special knowledge in Asia/Japan, don't look any further and contact me.
